History Of the club

Manchester United's story is utterly unique in sport, which is precisely why the club is revered and renowned all over the world. It's a head-spinning tale of triumphs – and tragedy – and a rich tradition of attacking, entertaining football, very often resulting in the kind of dramatic endings usually reserved for Hollywood blockbusters.
